# Legal Academic Writing

For law-related blog posts in this project:

1. Use formal, clear Spanish.
2. Do not invent legal facts or citations.
3. Preserve distinctions between:
   - legal norm
   - doctrine
   - jurisprudence
   - general explanation
4. Prefer precise wording over decorative writing.
5. Keep paragraph structure readable for university evaluation.

## Output checklist
- Clear title
- Intro
- Ordered sections with headings
- Conclusion when appropriate
- Neutral academic tone
